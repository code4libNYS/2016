C4L NYS 2016 Logistics

Where

Mann Library Rooms 160 (primary room) and 102 (workshop/breakout space). Mann Library is on the West side of the 'Agriculture Quad' on the Cornell University campus in Ithaca, New York. See the map below for getting around the Cornell campus.

When

Thursday and Friday, August 4-5, 2016. The schedule will run approximately 9 AM to 5 PM on both days, with a break for lunch on your own. A schedule will be released later this Spring, along with other logistical information.

Lodging

There are a few hotels and good AirBnB spots in the area. We are not designating a hotel or hotel block for this event, but are very happy to help with recommendations. Feel free to email Christina - see the Contact information below.

Getting to/from Ithaca and Cornell

Ithaca is in a beautiful part of the state, served by a small airport, some bus lines, and a few state roads (if driving). We will be working on getting recommended routes and directions in this section over the course of the Spring. There are also recommended parking spots and a good city bus system to help you in getting around the city while you're here.

Food, Drink & Entertainment

Ithaca has a great restaurant and nightlife scene. We will be working on getting some recommendations for you on the site in the near Spring.
